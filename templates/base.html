All of The Gentle Reader's selections (and any images) are from works in the public domain in the US. Old books. (If a source text is not, in fact, in the public domain, there has been a misunderstanding; please inform The Editor and it will be taken down.)

The fonts are Crimson, by Sebastian Kosch, and IM Fell English with some ornaments from IM Fell Flowers 2, both digitally reproduced by Iginio Marini. I would like to publicly thank these benefactors.
